## Building A Lens of Truth

This post introduces a short, self-contained paper titled **“Building A Lens of Truth.”**

The paper does not propose a theory of truth, nor does it attempt to resolve philosophical debates about meaning, belief, or correctness. Its purpose is narrower and more disciplined: to describe how a *lens* may be constructed that reveals what must remain invariant, what fails, and what must translate in any system that preserves coherence.

Truth, in this posture, is not treated as a property of statements or beliefs. It is treated as what survives coherence-preserving transformation.

The paper is intentionally brief. It is designed to transfer meaning rather than provide instruction, methodology, or examples. Nothing in it asks for agreement, adoption, or belief.
